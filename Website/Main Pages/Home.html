General information about Sri Lanka

Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, Formaly Known as Ceylon, is a Multi-cultural Country and an island country in South Asia. This country has history of more than 2500 years. some more general information as follows

  1. Ethanics : Sinhalese, Tamil, Muslims, Burghers
  2. Religions : Buddhism, Christianity, Hinduism, Islam
  3. Extent : 65,610 sq km
  4. Official Language : Sinhala
  5. Capital : Sri Jayawardenapura Kotte
  6. Population : 21.92 Million
  7. Administrative divisions : 9 Provinces & 25 Districts
